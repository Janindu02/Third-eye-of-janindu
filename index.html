Welcome to the Third Eye of Janindu!

Hi, I'm Janindu Amaraweera, a passionate Photographer and Visual Storyteller dedicated to capturing moments that blend emotion and artistry.

Having completed the CameraLK Photography Course, I've developed the skills to master framing, composition, and storytelling. I specialize in wedding photography, pre-shoots, birthday shoots, nature photography, and capturing day-to-day events or anything that speaks to my heart as art.

Every photograph I take is a testament to my belief that beauty lies in the details, and I aim to transform your moments into timeless memories. Explore my work and let's create something extraordinary together.
